Clothes 4 U

at a glance

Company

Clothes 4U Tex Sourcing Ltd is one of the fastest growing apparel suppliers in Bangladesh, started in 2016 to provide in ready made garments industry world wide.
In fact we are not new in the industry, our journey started a decade ago as a fabric supplier. So, this vast experience is working as one of the driving forces to strengthen our position in the market.

Core Values

Our values are extremely important to us because we believe that they are crucial to making the difference to our customers and they can help to position the products and services in this competitive market. Therefore we would emphasise on the following values:

1. Be first to meet our customers needs.
2. Working together to build a long term relationship.
3. Maintaining quality of the products and services.
4. Trust and respect each other.

Mission

Our mission is to strengthen our business by focusing on consistency in quality while building strong long-term relationships with our customers.

Philosophy

How We Work

We love working together. We always focus on the commitment to Quality in consistency and customers satisfaction. Of course Clothes 4 U is also careful enough about its price integrity policy ( i.e. price is fair and reasonable ).
From our experiences we learnt one thing that time management and shipment process is very vital in apparel in industry so we are committed to deliver our products with due-diligence.
All products are thoroughly checked for their quality with C4U's meticulous care so that we can meet the requirements of our customers.
Every customer is unique to us. We respect and care our customers to establish a long term relationship.
