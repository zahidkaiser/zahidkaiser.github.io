We Are

Clothes4U

Clothes 4U Tex Sourcing Ltd is one of the fastest growing apparel suppliers in Bangladesh, providing for the international ready made garments industry. We like working together so, we try our level best to understand the needs and expectations of our customers.
We do a huge range of different products under knit, oven and sweater ( simple to complex and almost all basic and common items ) for men, women and children as well as uniforms or corporate wear and home textile.

Women's Wear

Basic women's shirts, pants, jackets, outer wear, sweaters, night wear, paijama set etc are the generic product lines for women. On top of this we develop sample and design for our customers.

Men's Wear

Our range in men's wear is really vast. We produce almost all basic shirts, jackets, trousers and sweaters for men ie. t shirts, polo shirts, shorts, long pants etc. We try our best to fulfil the requirements of our customers.

Kids' Wear

We make a wide range of basic items for the kids of different ages including trousers, shirts, jacket, outer wear, night wear, summer and winter clothes and others. Our experience merchandisers are always ready to help with pricing, design, and developing sample.

Corporate Wear / Uniforms

This is one of our rich department. We produce almost all basic corporate wears and uniforms ( casual shirts, polo shirts, trousers, uniforms ) for different companies and schools.

Home Textile

We also supply various items in home textiles ie. quilt covers, towels, pillowcases, bedsheets, curtains etc.
